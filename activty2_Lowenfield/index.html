Why vist japan?

It's a country that has something for everyone, They are one of the few counties that are good at cultivating past history and making technological advances to better society. If you are a fan of the traditional Japanese culture the countryside is full of nature, castles, agriculture, sakura blooms, etc. But if you are a fan of technology/ modern Japanese culture the inner cities like Shibuya will keep you engaged. From Shibuya to Hiroshima Castle japan has the perfect mix of modern and traditional values it will be a great trip for everyone.
